Sookjai means:

Sookjai is a Thai word that literally translates to "Happy Heart". The play with the school name's kerning, leading, tracking illustrates the playful nature of the school.

I hand drew this treble clef using the Apple pen on an iPad pro using the Adobe Draw application. After a dozen of different rendition, I settled on stylish-minimalistic clef. This reflects the slim and simple nature of the music school.

The imperfect perfect circle reflects the holistic teaching philosophy that Sookjai Music uses. While a circle signifies perfection, perfection is not what the school aims to mold the students to be. This is the reason why the circle is imperfect. This is also the reason why other elements are not centered in the circle.
